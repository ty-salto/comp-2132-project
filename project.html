Game Instructions

Movement:

  • W = Move Forward
  • S = Move Backward
  • A = Move Left
  • D = Move Right

Dice Battle:

Move Towards the monster to trigger the Dice battle.

Mechanics:

  • Win = Monster will die.
  • Lose = Player will respawn to starting point.
  • Draw = Roll Dice Again

Hang Man:

Move Towards the door to trigger the Hang man game.

Before Hangman can be triggered, Monsters in the level should be defeated

Mechanics:

  • You have only 6 incorrect guesses
  • Win = Door will open
  • Lose = Player will respawn to starting point.
